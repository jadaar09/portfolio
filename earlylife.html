What Was My Early Life Like?

When I was younger I was a pretty good child. I listened to my parents and did what I was supposed to do...usually. I remeber a few
times when I was what some would call "mischevious". I remeber one day my sister Ashley was watching me while our mom was at work. We were
in the living room and she said she wa sgoing upstairs for a minute. When she went upstairs, I went into the kitchen and got some scisiors
and cut my hair. The night before, my mom had washed and braided my hair so she could put into a style when she go back home from work.
I cut two braids off and threw them in the trash and put the scisirs on the counter. A few minutes later my sister came back into the
kitchen and asked me what happened. I just looked silently. She looked in the trash and saw my hair, I said "It fell out.". She called our
mom and started crying, thinking she would get in trouble. My mom was really chill about the situation and asked if I was ok, which I was,
so she said "Well it's hair. It'll grow back. I'll see y'all when I get home.". My sister just looked at me and walked away.

Another memory I favor is when I got to go on a study abroad trip to Europe. Specifically Milan, Itally; Nice, France; and Barcelona,
