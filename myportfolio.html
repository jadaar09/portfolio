Who is Jada Richardson?

Biography

My name is Jada Alexis Richardson. I was born on September 24, 2002 at the Washington Hospital Center. I was born into a family
consisting of a mother, father, aunt, and older sister. My mother, Judy, had my sister, Ashley, 11 years before having me. I am my
mother's second child, but my father's only child. On my mother's side I have 3 aunts: Avery, Kim, and Kathy; though Avery, Auntie A,
is who I am closest to out of all of them. My sister and I were pretty close growing up but we had our fare share of disputes, just
like all siblings.
